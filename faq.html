Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, services, and policies

How can we help you today?

How long does shipping take?

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days
  • Same-Day Delivery: Available for local orders placed before 2 PM
  • Store Pickup: Ready in 1 hour (available at select locations)

Shipping times are calculated from the moment your order is processed. You'll receive tracking information via email once your order ships.

Do you offer repair warranties?

Yes, all our repair services come with a comprehensive warranty:

  • 90-Day Service Warranty: Covers both parts and labor
  • Genuine Parts Guarantee: We use only original or certified replacement parts
  • Water Damage Warranty: 30-day warranty on water damage repairs
  • Screen Replacement Warranty: 180-day warranty on all screen repairs

If you experience any issues with your repair within the warranty period, bring your device back for a free diagnosis and repair.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most products:

  • Unopened Products: Full refund within 30 days
  • Opened Products: Return within 14 days for exchange or store credit
  • Defective Products: 1-year warranty from date of purchase
  • Special Items: Software, gift cards, and personalized items are non-returnable

To initiate a return, visit our Returns Center or contact our customer support team.

Are your products covered by manufacturer warranties?

Yes, all products sold through XS Platform are covered by manufacturer warranties:

  • Electronics: Typically 1-year manufacturer warranty
  • Apple Products: 1-year limited warranty + AppleCare eligibility
  • Extended Warranties: Available for purchase on most products
  • International Warranty: Most major brands offer global warranty coverage

We also offer our own 1-year extended warranty on all products for additional peace of mind.

How can I track my order?

There are several ways to track your order:

  1. Order Tracking Page: Visit our Order Tracking page and enter your order number and email
  2. Account Dashboard: Log into your account and view order history
  3. Email Notifications: You'll receive shipping confirmation with tracking link
  4. Mobile App: Track orders through our mobile application

If you're having trouble tracking your order, contact our support team for assistance.

How long does a typical repair take?

Repair times vary depending on the service:

  • Screen Replacements: 1-2 hours (same-day service available)
  • Battery Replacements: 30-60 minutes
  • Water Damage Repair: 24-48 hours (requires diagnostics and drying)
  • Software Issues: 1-2 hours
  • Motherboard Repair: 3-5 business days

We offer while-you-wait service for common repairs and provide loaner devices for longer repairs upon request.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a wide variety of payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
  • Digital Wallets: Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay
  • Bank Transfers: Available for business accounts
  • Financing: 0% APR financing available for purchases over $500
  • Cash: Accepted for in-store purchases
  • Cryptocurrency: Bitcoin and Ethereum accepted online

All payments are processed securely using industry-standard encryption.

Do you offer price matching?

Yes, we offer a 30-day price match guarantee:

  • Eligible Competitors: Major authorized retailers (Best Buy, Amazon, etc.)
  • Time Frame: Within 30 days of purchase
  • Requirements: Same product, new condition, in stock at competitor
  • Exclusions: Clearance items, refurbished products, limited-time sales

To request a price match, contact our customer service with proof of the lower price (screenshot or link).
