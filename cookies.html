What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

This Cookie Policy explains how XS Platform uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our Platform. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

By using XS Platform, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.

Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set, visit www.aboutcookies.org or www.allaboutcookies.org.

Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Chrome Cookie Settings
  • Mozilla Firefox: Firefox Cookie Settings
  • Safari: Safari Cookie Settings
  • Microsoft Edge: Edge Cookie Settings

Mobile Devices: You can also manage cookies on your mobile device by adjusting your device settings. The steps to do this vary by device and operating system.
